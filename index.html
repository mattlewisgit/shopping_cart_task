Calculator available at: https://mattlewisgit.github.io/shopping_cart_task/

Title: Shopping Cart

Description: As a Kingfisher user, I want to add products to basket, log in, and place an order to prove that my webpage works as expected.

Acceptance Criteria

  • I can add all the available products to basket.
  • I can remove a single product quantity from basket.
  • I can apply a discount using:
    • SAVE10 = 10% discount
    • SAVE20 = 20% discount
  • I can only checkout with the following credentials:
    • Username: user
    • Password: pass
  • I receive a checkout successful! Thank you for your purchase message.
